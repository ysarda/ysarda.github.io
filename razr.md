<h1> RASR </h1>

<h3> Based on the principle of high-level information fusion, RASR takes NOAA Doppler Weather data, and re-purposes it for meteor and entering object detection </h3>



<p>

RASR is an open source project based at The University of Texas at Austin, building on <a href="http://astria.tacc.utexas.edu/AstriaGraph/">ASTRIAGraph</a>. With almost complete coverage of the continental United States, it seeks to detect the velocity signatures of entering objects using computer vision, specifically the Matplotlib and OpenCV Python libraries. <br />
